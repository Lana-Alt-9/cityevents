Our Story

City Events Guide was born from a simple idea: every community deserves to know about the amazing events happening right in their backyard.

Founded in 2020, we started as a small team of event enthusiasts who were frustrated by the scattered information about local events. We believed there had to be a better way to discover concerts, festivals, workshops, and community gatherings.

Today, we're proud to be the leading platform for local event discovery, helping thousands of people find their next favorite experience every month.
