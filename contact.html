Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Listing your event is easy! Simply contact us through the form above with "List My Event" as the subject, or email us directly at partnerships@cityevents.com. We'll guide you through our simple submission process and help you reach more attendees.

We offer both free and premium listing options. Basic listings are completely free and include essential event information. Premium listings offer enhanced visibility, featured placement, and additional promotional tools for a small fee.

Purchasing tickets is secure and straightforward. Click on any event to view details, then select "Get Tickets" or "Purchase Tickets." You'll be guided through our secure checkout process. We accept all major credit cards and PayPal.

Refund policies vary by event organizer. Most events offer refunds if cancelled by the organizer. For event-specific refund policies, check the event details page or contact the organizer directly. We're happy to help facilitate communication if needed.

Subscribe to our newsletter for weekly event updates, follow us on social media, or create an account to set up personalized event alerts based on your interests and location. You can also bookmark events you're interested in.
